What Doctors actually do?

Dr. Brittany Filipetti collaborates with Athletic Therapists, Kinesiologists, Acupuncturists, Medical doctors, Physiotherapists, Registered Massage Therapists, and Naturopaths in the Comox Valley and Campbell River to find the most efficient and cost effective treatment plan for you.

How Doctor's get the treatment?

Doctor's finds the tissues that are dysfunctional and/or hypoxic and tight (lack oxygen due to poor circulation) and then use the techniques listed to get you better faster

Graston Technique

A stainless steel tool used to loosen up the tissue and breakdown myofascial adhesions.

Active Release Technique

Tension is placed on the muscle with hands as the patient stretches the muscle slowly.

Shockwave Therapy

Acoustic waves generated by the Shockwave trigger biological effects which lead to faster and long-term healing and regeneration of the tissue. Shockwave therapy (SWT) covers a wide range of indications such as tennis elbow, plantar fasciitis/osis, achilles tendinitis, and rotator cuff injuries. Shockwave Therapy coupled with Class IV Laser Therapy is evidence based and the gold standard in tendon healing.

Spinal and Extremity Adjustments/Manipulation

Traditional Chiropractic Adjustments. Alignment is important and the dysfunctional musculature can restrict the joints by pulling on them. Joints should be able to glide and absorb shock.

Class IV Deep Tissue Laser Therapy

Photon energy from the laser is converted into chemical energy in the mitochondria of the cell. Photons activate cytochrome c complex within the electron transport chain to produce ATP/energy. This increases microcirculation and accelerates healing at a cellular level.

Rock Tape

Rock Tape involves the skilled application of one continuous piece of kinesiology tape to muscle groups that need extra support and relief. We can use Rock Tape to diminish pain signals and prevent unhealthy posture.
